Fruit tree specialists in Bradford and Leeds

We make fruit growing easy as piss.

Fruit Works Co-operative is a social enterprise helping communities in Bradford and Leeds to plant and maintain fruit trees. Our mission is to enable more people to grow their own fruit as a step towards building resilient local food systems.

Learn More

Planting. Pruning. Propagating.

We provide a range of services based around the seasonal cycles of fruit trees. As well as doing this work ourselves, we are committed to sharing our knowledge and providing training to people new to growing fruit.
